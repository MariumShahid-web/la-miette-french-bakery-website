Our Story

A tiny bakery with big intentions.

La Miette began in 2008 as a one-oven shop on a quiet street. Today we still mill our own flour, ferment our doughs for 36 hours, and pull every pastry by hand before sunrise.

Our philosophy is simple: real ingredients, unhurried time, and the kind of warmth you can only get from a place that loves what it makes.
